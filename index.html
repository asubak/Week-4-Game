Crystal Collector

Instructions

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game, the score to match.

  2. There are four cyrstals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the "score to match" number and you will lose the game if your total score goes above the "score to match" number.

  4. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it and it adds to your total score. Keep track of what each crystal is worth for each game!

  5. Each time the game starts, so after you win or lose, the game will change the values of each crystal to keep you on your toes.
